## Tech Stuff

I decided to use [Astro](https://astro.build/) as the general framework with
[MDX](https://mdxjs.com/) for most of the content pages. I decided on it for the ease of use and
simplicity that they provide in general but with the added benefit of being powerful enough for
complex things when I need them to be.

I had already decided to host this website on
[Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com/products/workers/) before Astro was acquired by them for no
particular reason other than that they seem to have a better free tier than
[Github Pages](https://docs.github.com/en/pages) and I generally heard good things about them. Note
I have not tried other hosting service so take this opinion with a grain of salt.

I started to use [Vite+](https://viteplus.dev/) for managing the project. It's promise of a unified
toolchain was really enticing to me and since this was a rather simple project I thought it would be
perfect to test drive it. Its still in early development as of writing but overall I'm pretty
satified though for more mature/complex project I would suggest not migratting just yet.

## Design

I tried to go for a minimalistic asthetic and make the user experience feel as smooth as possible. I
tried to make the website with little to no visual clutter when it came to major text sections. The
reading experience is very important to me and I tried to make it so people can actually enjoy
reading on this website for a long period of time(if the wrtiing is interesting).

### Typography

- **Exo 2** -- A geometric sans-serif typeface with a futuristic style that I use for all
  headings/titles. I could probably find a much better font for the headings but a good friend of
  mine from a long time ago used this font a lot and because of that I have a bias towards using it.
- **Share Tech** -- Another techno style font that acts a lot like a monospace font but isn't. Its
  mainly used for text that isn't meant to be the main focus like UI elements.
- **Atkinson Hyperlegible Next** -- This font might be the most readable font I've encountered and
  is really nice to write blogs in. Its main text you'll see throughout the website.
